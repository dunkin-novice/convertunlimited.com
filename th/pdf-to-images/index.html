How PDF to Images Works

ConvertUnlimited uses PDF.js, a powerful rendering library developed by Mozilla, to decode PDF documents directly in your browser. Our tool iterates through every page of your PDF, draws it onto an HTML5 Canvas at your selected resolution (scale), and then exports that canvas as a high-quality image file.

Because the rendering happens entirely on your own device's CPU and RAM, your sensitive documents never leave your machine. This local-first approach provides 100% privacy and allows for fast extraction of pages even from large PDF files.

When to Convert PDF Pages to Images?

  • Social Media Sharing: Easily post specific pages of a report or document as images on platforms that don't support PDF uploads.
  • Web Presentations: Embed document pages directly into blog posts or websites as standard image files for better browser compatibility.
  • Extracting Content: Quickly grab a specific graph, chart, or text section from a long PDF without needing complex PDF editing software.
  • Organization: Bundle extracted pages as a ZIP for easy archival or distribution.

Resolution and Render Scale Explained

PDF documents are vector-based, meaning they can be rendered at any size. Our tool offers three scales:

  • Standard (1x): Renders at the document's original intended size. Ideal for mobile-friendly thumbnails.
  • High (2x): Double the resolution. This is our recommended setting for crisp, readable text and clear photos.
  • Extra High (3x): Triple resolution. Best for large prints or detailed diagrams where every pixel matters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert encrypted PDFs?

Yes, provided you have the password. Your browser will prompt you to enter the password to unlock the document before the rendering begins. The password is never sent to our servers.

Will my images lose quality?

By choosing the "High" or "Extra High" render scales, you ensure that the extracted images maintain the sharp quality of the original PDF. Output formats like WebP and JPG at 90% quality preserve excellent detail.
