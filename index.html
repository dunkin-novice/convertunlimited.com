How it works

Drop in your images, pick a format, and ConvertUnlimited converts them with the canvas API right in your browser. Nothing is uploaded — your files never leave your device. Save individually, or grab everything as a ZIP.

FAQ

Are there any limits? No software-imposed cap. We suggest ~50 images at a time so your browser stays responsive.

Are my files uploaded? No. Conversion happens locally with the HTML canvas API — files never leave your device.

Will big batches slow my computer? Yes — converting many images uses your CPU and memory. Use at your own discretion.

Why is it free? Ads cover hosting and keep the tool unlimited. That's it.

Privacy

Your images. ConvertUnlimited converts images entirely inside your browser. No file is ever uploaded to a server, and we never see, store, or transmit your images.

Ads. We use Google AdSense to keep the tool free. Google and its ad partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads and measure performance. You can manage personalised-ad settings at google.com/settings/ads.

Analytics. We do not run third-party analytics or fingerprinting on this site.

Contact. Questions about privacy? Reach out via the project's GitHub.
